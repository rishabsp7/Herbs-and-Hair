Our Story

Our Journey

Herbs and Hair began in the kitchen as a remedy for my postpartum hair fall, when all else failed.

After 12 years of adding and removing ingredients to form the perfect blend, for my daughter and family, by the age of 45, I found my calling.

I felt a deep desire to provide a solution to people who face the same issues related to hair, and thus, Herbs and Hair was born-a gift from a mother to every person in search of something truly nourishing.

"A gift from a mother to every person in search of something truly nourishing."

— Jufreena S P, Founder
